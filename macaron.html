How To Make Macaron!

Welcome To my Page! This how to site will help you make Macarons!

Recipe

You will need:

  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 2 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

    Instructions

  1. Combine almond flour and confectioner's sugar in food processor
  2. sift the sugar and flour combination
  3. In a bowl, beat egg whites until frothy:then gradualy add in the granulated sugar and vanilla
  4. beat until stiff peaks form
  5. Fold half of dry ingredients in; then fold in the second half
  6. place mixture in piping bag
  7. pipe 1 inch an inch away from each other on a baking sheet covered in parchment paper.
  8. tap the sheets against counter until bubbles release
  9. let them dry until batter no longer sticks to finger when touched
  10. Preheat oven to 325
  11. back for about 10 minutes but watch them so that they don't burn
  12. let cool
  13. make filling of choice
  14. after they are cool match up like sized cookies and add filling
  15. sandwich cookies together
