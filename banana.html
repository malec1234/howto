How To Make Banana Bread!

Welcome To my Page! This how to site will help you make Banana Bread!

Banana bread is so fun to bake. I am so happy to show you the way to make banana bread.The experience of baking has been life changing for me. I hope you enjoy!

Recipe

You will need:

  • 3 ripe bananas mashed with some milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of oil

    Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. mix sugar,oil, and egg in a bowl
  3. next add mashed bananas
  4. next add flour, baking soda, and baking powder, and salt
  5. Divide batter evenly in pan
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
  7. cool and serve
