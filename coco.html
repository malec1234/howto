How To Make No crust Coconut pie!

Welcome To my Page! This how to site will help you make Coconut pie!

Coconut pie is so fun to bake. I am so happy to show you the way to make Coconut pie.The experience of baking has been life changing for me. I hope you enjoy!

Recipe

You will need:

  • 3/4 cup's Margarine
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 2 tablspoons of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablspoon of vaniila flavor
  • 3 1/2 oz can of flamed Coconut

    Instructions

  1. Melt margarine & mix well with sugar
  2. add eggs all at once( beat eggs well first)
  3. Add milk, flour, and flavor
  4. add all together, eggs, sugar, coconut, etc./li>
  5. add coconut last,
  6. Pour into greased pan
  7. Bake at 375 degrees for 30-40 minutes to 1 hour or until golden brown
  8. Cool and serve
