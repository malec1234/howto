Recipe

You will need:

  • 3 cups of sugar
  • 1 cup better of Margarine
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups of plain sifted flour

    Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Cream Butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  3. add eggs one at a time
  4. beat in sour cream alternsately with flour,salt,soda(shifted together)
  5. add flavors and pour into pan
  6. bake for 60 minutes or until done
  7. Cool and serve
