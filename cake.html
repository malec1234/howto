Recipe

You will need:

  • Store bought Graham cracker crust pans
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream

    Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. put crust pans on baking sheet
  3. beat the cream cheese until it is fluffy
  4. beat in sour cream/li>
  5. beat in eggs,vanilla,
  6. Divide batter evenly between pans/li>
  7. Bake for 18- 20 minutes
