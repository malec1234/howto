Recipe

You will need:

  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup confectionersâ€™ sugar
  • 2 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

    Instructions

  • Combine almond flour and confectioner's sugar in food processor
  • sift the sugar and flour combination
  • In a bowl, beat egg whites until frothy:then gradualy add in the granulated sugar and vanilla
  • beat until stiff peaks form
  • Fold half of dry ingredients in; then fold in the second half
  • place mixture in piping bag
  • pipe 1 inch an inch away from each other on a baking sheet covered in parchment paper.
  • tap the sheets against counter until bubbles release
  • let them dry until batter no longer sticks to finger when touched
  • Preheat oven to 325
  • back for about 10 minutes but watch them so that they don't burn
  • let cool
  • make filling of choice
  • after they are cool match up like sized cookies and add filling

Tips

  • If cookies stick pour water underneath parchment paper
  • Make filling during drying time
  • make sure that room where drying macaron is not humid
